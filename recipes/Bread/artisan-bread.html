Artizan Bread

Homemade Artisan bread that provides the tast and texture you love without a complicated recipe.

Ingredients

  • Bread Flour
  • Instant Yeast
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Optional: Cornmeal

Steps

  1. Mix the dough ingredients together.
  2. Let it rise at room temperature for 2-3 hours.
  3. Use right away or refrigerate
  4. Shape into 2 loaves or 1 boule. Rest as oven preheats.
  5. Bake until golden brown, about 20-25 minutes.
